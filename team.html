Our Team

The history of the Fatafat Pots mark goes back to little shop in 1998, Delhi where we were home to Delhi's pressure cooker needs. Fatafat Pots gained the trust of thousands of households and as the time passed, we expanded to various best in class Rice Cookers which gave birth to the name Fatafat Pots.
